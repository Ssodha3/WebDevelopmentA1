The Ferrari California T first uncovered on the web on February 12, 2014. To highlight the TURBO technology, last used in F40 road-car this name was selected where T stands for Turbo. It is one of the first car which had the Apple CarPlay functionality build-in the system.This model is Designed under the direction of Flavio Manzoni in Ferrari Styling Centre collaborating with Pininfarina. Price typically started at around $200,000 dollars.It reduced emission pollution by 15%. Its features are listed below:

  • Fuel economy of 13 mpg in the city and 19 mpg on the highway and engine of 3.9 L F154 BB twin-turbo V8.
  • Leather upholstery and interior trim & retractable Hardtop Convertible along with braking capability by Carbon-Ceramic Brakes
  • Advanced infotainment system with navigation and multimedia functions with various driver assistance features.
